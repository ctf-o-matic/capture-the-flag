Rules

  • Form teams of 2-4 people.
  • The winner is the team to reach the highest level the fastest.
  • The winner is expected to be able to explain the solution of all levels.
  • Each level implies a lesson to learn in software security. Try to identify what it is!
  • Hints will be released daily.
  • There are no trick questions. The key to the next level is always about the vulnerable code, not something else, and not the environment, or this leaderboard.

Warnings

A team may be disqualified in case of abuses.

  • Please do not try to abuse the competition environment and the leaderboard.
  • Please do not try to harm other participants.
  • Please do not reveal the solutions to other teams or publicly, during or after this competition.
