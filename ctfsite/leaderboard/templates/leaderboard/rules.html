Rules

  • Form teams of 2-4 people.
    • Team members cannot change after the team has passed level0.
    • The winner is the team to reach the highest level the fastest.
    • The winner must be able to explain the solution of all levels, or else forfeit victory.
  • Each level implies a lesson to learn in software security. Try to identify what it is!
  • Hints will be released daily.
  • There are no trick questions. The key to the next level is always about the vulnerable code of that level. It is not a hidden objective to root the system, or the leaderboard, or to sniff network packets of other users.

Warnings

Users are not isolated in the competition environment.

  • CPU, memory, disk I/O intensive operations may get terminated.
  • Any file you create may get read / modified / deleted by other users.
  • The content of /tmp is wiped out every hour.

A team may be disqualified in case of abuses.

  • Please do not try to abuse the competition environment and the leaderboard.
  • Please do not try to harm other participants.
  • Please do not reveal the solutions to other teams or publicly, during or after this competition.
